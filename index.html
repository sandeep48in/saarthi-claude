About Sarathi

Sarathi is your universal travel guardian — built to plan ahead, watch over you, and help when things go sideways.

Free, offline-capable, no accounts. Powered by Open-Meteo, OpenStreetMap, and your browser's geolocation.

Medical disclaimer: The symptom checker offers general guidance only and is not a substitute for professional medical advice. In emergencies, contact local services immediately.
